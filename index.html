There’s A Disconnect in Data Science


Aspiring Data Scientists have a wealth of resources to get the technical skills they need: tutorials, blog posts, podcasts, bootcamps, online courses, MOOCs, even university degrees.
However, when they search for and apply for positions in the field, they find that entry-level positions require experience!

Hiring Managers and Recruiters have many data science requirements to fill but lack qualified candidates that have real-world experience.
Additionally, companies may not have the time or resources to train brand new data scientists.

Many organizations, such as startups and small-to-medium sized businesses, want to apply data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning to their businesses but don’t have the budget for a full-time team of data scientists.

Method Data Science bridges this gap.
